📋 Event Rules

⏱
15-Min Peace Time After every event, no robbing or hostile RP for 15 minutes.
🎬
Always Be Clipping Clip software must be running at all events. No clips = no returns.
🟢
GZ Events Event locations are green zones for the duration + buffer time.
💀
KOS Events Airdrops and Cayo are KOS inside the zone only. No robbing inside.
📢
All Announcements in Discord Times, locations, and themes are posted in the announcements channel before every event.
