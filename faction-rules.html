📋 FACTION BOSSES, READ THIS FIRST. It is your responsibility to ensure ALL of your members are reading, following, and understanding the rules. This includes both the Faction Rulebook and the Empire Rulebook.

⚠️ IGNORANCE OF THE RULES IS NOT AN EXCUSE. By joining a faction you confirm you have read all rules and will be held accountable. If Faction Support catches you violating the Faction Rulebook or any Empire RP guidelines, you may be subject to removal.
📃
General Rules
Core rules and expectations for all faction members
▾
City RulesAll factions must follow all city rules listed in the Empire Rulebook at all times.
AlliancesFaction alliances are NOT permitted. There is no teaming up or shooting alongside opposing factions or civilians.
⚠️ YOU MUST FOLLOW THE EMPIRE RULEBOOK. You must also follow all rules in the official Empire Rulebook at all times, in addition to this Faction Rulebook. Any rule breaks will be handled per the city/Empire guidelines, and will also be punished with a Strike under the Strike punishment ladder in this rulebook.
Staff ConductDisrespecting staff will NOT be tolerated. Oversight Leads have the right to FA (Free Agent) a member for staff disrespect, at their discretion. If you feel an FA for staff disrespect was wrong, or that the offense wasn't a big enough deal to warrant it, you may open a staff complaint (staff comm) to have it reviewed.
Harassment"Running a faction out of the city" is considered harassment. Any attempt to force another faction to leave the city will be treated as harassing an individual and will result in punishment. This applies to both in-city and out-of-city interactions.
ExploitingReloading skin is NOT allowed — treated as exploiting. B-reloading is NOT allowed — treated as exploiting. Binding emotes to GTA keybinds to gain an advantage is considered exploiting.
Power GamingOnce an interaction has begun, you may NOT access your stash for any reason. Stashing items during an interaction is power gaming. Tackling is NOT allowed — considered power gaming.
RegistrationYou may NOT flag up until Faction Support has assigned roles, an updated roster screenshot is submitted, and roles are officially given in Faction Discord.
8v8 RuleFactions are limited to 8 active participants per side in any scene (shootouts, criminal activities, car chases). Any extra members MUST disengage and leave the area — they cannot assist, act as lookout, or provide callouts. If you have more than 8 and are trying to avoid interaction in the area of the interaction, you will still get punished with over T-count — leave the area! Once you have flatbacked, you can hold E.
Fail RPDeliberately flatbacking your own faction members to prevent them from being robbed is strictly prohibited and WILL be considered Fail RP.
RoofsRoofs are NOT allowed under any circumstances — block pushes, street fights, fights vs PD or civilians. The only exception is if specifically listed in an event.
WeedWeed is a civilian drug. You may go there to get weed to sell, but there is NO taxing anyone for weed.
Factions + CivsFactions are NOT permitted to team up with civilians under any circumstances (killing, robbing, any criminal activity). You can farm drugs together, but if civilians start getting pressed by other civs/gangs, your gang must not interfere, and vice versa.
🚫 DO NOT F8 DURING AN ACTIVE SCENE. Your pockets/items WILL be taken if you disconnect during a scene. You will be handled for an F8 offense. Repeated situations may lead to gang-wide consequences.
What counts as an F8: Leaving during a shooting or hostile interaction · Disconnecting to avoid being robbed, captured, or killed · Crashing intentionally to avoid RP outcomes.
If you genuinely crash: Return as quickly as possible and communicate with involved players or staff. Staff will determine the situation fairly.
EMSEMS must be allowed to safely perform their duties without interference during active scenes. Once EMS is cleared to enter the scene, all fighting must immediately stop. No continued shootings, punching, or hostile actions while EMS is actively treating or transporting players. If EMS arrives while the scene is still ongoing, inform EMS it is still active and wait until fighting officially ends. NOT allowed: shooting while EMS treats players, using EMS as cover, or attacking EMS personnel.

Greenzone Rules
Safe zones, no hostile scenes allowed
▾
🌿 GREENZONES ARE SAFE ZONES, NO ACTIVE SCENES. Greenzones (GZs) are controlled safe areas. No hostile scenes should be started or escalated inside a Greenzone.
No ViolenceInitiating or having hostile scenes inside greenzones is prohibited. Escalating interactions into shootings, fights, or killings is prohibited. Using a greenzone interaction to immediately start a scene afterward is prohibited.
InteractionIf you approach another gang inside a GZ, the interaction must remain non-violent. Talking trash inside a GZ is fine, do NOT take it further than that.
PurposeGreenzones provide safe areas around the city and prevent unnecessary conflict in public spaces. They keep RP organized and fair for everyone. There are rarely valid RP reasons for violent scenes inside a Greenzone.
Clothing StoresDo NOT try to kill or take hostage any member who is actively in a clothing menu. Do NOT camp outside clothing stores waiting for them to come outside.
⚠️ FAILURE TO FOLLOW GREENZONE RULES MAY RESULT IN: Punishment and scene termination · Gang-wide consequences depending on the situation.

POV / Clipping Rules
Recording requirements, requests, and failure consequences
▾
GeneralAll kills must be clipped and kept for 48 hours. Only Bosses and Underbosses can request POV from the other bosses. Faction Support may NOT discuss possible cheaters, punishments, or irrelevant information with other faction members.
Clip SharingAll POVs must be sent to #clip-request, this channel is only so the Boss and Underboss can see it. If you'd like to request a POV, send the request to your Boss or Underboss, they will @ the faction involved. Clips are NOT allowed to be saved and sent around in faction chat, only if it's been requested can a clip be sent to your faction.
Invalid POVThe following will automatically invalidate your POV: No props, no vegetation, no water, loud footsteps, and no day and night cycle in the clip · Missing HUD elements (health bar, stamina bar, or other HUD stats) · Any rule-break by the player requesting or providing POV.
AudioAll audio must be clearly audible, game audio AND microphone audio. If Faction Support determines audio cannot be clearly heard, the POV will be automatically denied.
OversightPOV requests are at the sole discretion of Oversight. If required, you MUST provide it. Players are NOT entitled to receiving another player's POV.
RequestingClip must be a minimum of 60 seconds: 50 seconds leading up to your death + 10 seconds after. Your POV must clearly show your death, POV will NOT be requested if you did not die. You must request POV within 4 hours of your death. Requests submitted after 4 hours are automatically denied.
ProvidingClip must be a minimum of 60 seconds: 50 seconds leading up to your death/kill + 10 seconds after. You have 12 hours from when Faction Support requests your POV to provide it. Failure to submit within 1 hour = punishment at Oversight discretion. Any disrespect, refusal, or arguing with staff regarding a POV request is treated as failure to provide POV.

▾

🚫 PETTY TICKETS WILL NOT BE TOLERATED. Submitting tickets without a valid rule violation · Wasting staff time with minor issues · Spiteful or retaliatory reports. Possible punishments: Community Service, Discord timeouts, and more.

Faction Basics
Territory, Empire Games, Warzone, and Clothing rules
▾
🗺️ TERRITORIES ARE CONTROLLED BY ACTIVITY, NOT KILLS. Each action a gang member performs inside a zone increases their control percentage. This is NOT a kill zone, killing another gang will NOT gain territory. You must follow interaction guidelines.
TerritoryWays to build control: Graffiti Tagging (coming soon) · Selling Drugs, completing sales in a zone increases influence, and selling in your territory also increases your drug payout. Control of a zone shifts dynamically based on which gang is most active there.
Empire GamesEvery month there will be one event at the end of the month (rotating format, RP, Hunger Games style, etc.).
WarzoneUnlimited T-count allowed. Grenades and sticky bombs are allowed. Only consumables are to be robbed. Everyone MUST stay in the zone, going out of zone will result in you or your team getting /killed. Everyone will be starting inside a helicopter piloted by staff. Once the helicopter gets into the zone everyone is free to jump out. If you fail to deploy the parachute, you will be picked up wherever you landed.
ClothingFaction colors/flags must be worn AT ALL TIMES unless working at a whitelisted business or doing a heist. Pretending to wear another faction's colors or flag to confuse others is a strike. All faction-branded clothing must be approved by Faction Oversight before being imported into the city. We are NOT responsible for unapproved clothing, get approval before purchasing. Factions must wear 50% of their primary colors AT ALL TIMES!

Starting a Faction
Whitelist requirements and creation process
▾
NameFaction Name, No fighting over names. Must not break TOS, must be original. No 'cartel', 'mafia', or real-life gang/organization names.
ColorsFaction Colors, No fighting over colors. Must be approved by Faction Oversight.
BlockFaction Block, No fighting over blocks. Must be approved by Faction Oversight.
RosterUp to 25 members (including boss). No more than 1 boss, up to 2 underbosses, 2 captains. Minimum 6 members to form and remain a faction. Custom flag required (temp flag allowed initially, but custom flag must be purchased eventually).
Creation ProcessFill out the faction application, Faction Oversight will determine if your faction is a good fit for the city. Open a faction support ticket to begin the process after completing the application. Faction Oversight has the right to deny any application deemed unfit.
Faction LoreYour lore must describe how your faction came to be and how it will grow in the city. Minimum 500 words, 0% AI. Low-quality or low-effort responses may result in being asked to rewrite, or an outright denial. Not following the lore you create may result in disbandment.
🛡️ NEW FACTION, 1-WEEK PROTECTION OPTION. New factions may activate a 1-week protection period to get started without outside attacks. During protection: you CANNOT press other groups or be caught at high-risk spots like drug areas. Breaking these rules ends your protection immediately.

Leaving a Faction
Free Agent status, removals, and cooldowns
▾
Requesting FAYou must be bled out by a captain or above with a clip proving the bleed out! Standard cooldown is 5 days. You may NOT join another faction until your cooldown is complete OR you are approved to use/purchase a CD skip.
RemovalBosses/Underbosses have the right to remove anyone at any time. Cooldown for removal is 1 week (7 days).
🚫 DURING YOUR COOLDOWN YOU MAY NOT: Participate in faction activities · Wear faction colors/flags · Use faction radio · Represent a faction in any capacity.
Cooldown BuyoutYou may pay 80k per day to reduce your cooldown.

Disbanding
Voluntary, forced, and cheating-related disbandment rules
▾
GeneralIf a faction boss disbands, all members receive a 5-day cooldown before joining or creating another faction. Instead of disbanding, the boss may transfer ownership to another approved member. If a faction is fully disbanded, all Tebex items tied to that faction are permanently lost. After disbandment, the faction name is locked for 1 week and MAY be reused.
Forced DisbandmentForced disbandments may occur due to: excessive faction strikes (typically 3+), inactive faction leadership, or other disciplinary reasons. After forced disbandment, the boss may be prohibited from creating another faction in Empire, at Management discretion.
☠️ CHEATING-RELATED DISBANDMENTS: 30-day cooldown for the faction · Leadership may receive faction restrictions based on involvement · Players directly involved with the cheating may face additional punishment, including faction blacklisting at Oversight's discretion. Punishments are determined on a case-by-case basis. Members who were not involved may be exempt from additional penalties.
VoluntaryOnly the faction boss may request voluntary disbandment. Cooldown is 5 days (can be paid down).
Support DisbandmentIf Faction Support disbands the gang due to rule breaks or other circumstances, all members receive a 1-week cooldown.

Robbing & Returning Items
When you can and cannot rob other players
▾
PermittedFaction members may rob a player under the following conditions: Incapacitation — if a player is on their side, they may be robbed · If your faction member is still on their side, you may request their items · If you are already in the robbing animation and the player you are robbing flatbacks, you can rob them. Double dipping is not allowed.
ProhibitedFaction members may NOT rob a player under the following conditions: If someone is complying to a rob, you cannot incapacitate them (put them on their side) · You may not rob a player who is flatbacked · You may not rob someone and then flatback them · You may not flatback your own members (to prevent them from being robbed, or any other reason) · If your faction member is flatbacked, you may not rob them for their items.
🚫 YOU CANNOT POCKET WIPE — meaning you can't take phone and radio!
Returning ItemsStaff reserve the right to retrieve items directly from a player's stash.

Verified Beef System
Creating valid beef through RP before interacting with factions
▾
Getting BeefFactions will need to create valid beef through RP in order to interact with other factions. Verified beef resets between factions every 2 weeks. You may have verified beef with more than one faction at a time. Once beef has been reset, you must create new beef. You may not interact with a faction you do not have verified beef with. You still need to log every cooldown in your cooldowns channel.
⚠️ WEEKLY REQUIREMENT: You need a minimum of 4 verified beefs from different factions. You must get 4 separate verified beef scenes with 4 different factions per week — this is a requirement or it will result in a gang strike.
SubmittingYou must open a faction support ticket providing a 2-minute clip leading up to the interaction. The faction team will either validate or deny the beef depending on the interaction.
Step 1 — First InteractionNo guns · No robbing · Fists/melees only · Must include quality RP · Cannot end in a shootout.
Step 2 — Verification2-minute lead-up clip · Clear audio · Full, unedited interaction · No rule breaks · Submit via Faction Support Ticket.
Step 3 — After ApprovalSecond interaction & interactions following after may escalate to firearms. Robbing is allowed. All standard faction rules apply.
First InteractionThe first interaction may NOT end in a shootout or with the use of guns — fists or melees only, NO ROBBING. The second interaction may end in a shootout & robbing is allowed. Quality RP is required to validate beef.
ℹ️ ACCOUNTABILITY: Factions engaging in quality RP will not be held accountable for the opposing faction's rule-breaks and/or lack of intent to roleplay or intent to avoid getting verified beef. Lack of intent to RP or rule-breaks may include: Avoiding Interaction · Overall Failure to Roleplay · Pulling guns out on the first interaction · Not having valid beef & engaging in gunfights. Faction Oversight will allow verified beef to be given between two factions regardless of one faction's lack of RP — if the engaging faction does everything right on their side, beef will be announced.

Block Fights
Rules for pushing a faction's home block
▾
📋 MANDATORY RULES WHEN PUSHING A FACTION BLOCK. Minimum 60-second good roleplay interaction before a shootout can occur. Weapons CANNOT be drawn during the interaction period, even if you are on your own block.
T-CountIf pushed on your block, you are allowed unlimited T-Count within a 2-street radius of your home block. Once outside that 2-street radius, T-Count is reduced from unlimited to 8.
BackupYou can call for backup on block to let the opposing gang know you have more coming. They are no longer able to pull up once guns are drawn!

On-Foot & Avoiding
Interaction timer, pursuit rules, and avoiding classification
▾
TimerInteraction must last a minimum of 60 seconds, conducted clearly and verbally giving the opposing faction sufficient time to acknowledge and respond.
PursuitIf the opposing faction flees, you may pursue for up to 10 seconds before drawing weapons. After 10 seconds of continued evasion, you are permitted to shoot to disable the vehicle.
Safe ZonesPlayers are strictly prohibited from fleeing into or initiating within businesses, greenzones, or clothing stores. This is considered powergaming and may result in disciplinary action.
AvoidingRunning in circles or not replying until the interaction timer expires is classified as FAIL RP and will be handled accordingly.
⚠️ NO DEATH CIRCLE! When the interaction starts, everyone must stand still and not move around.

Street Fights
Rules for fights away from your home block
▾
No RetreatIf you have an interaction in the streets, you CANNOT run back to block.
No SetupNO setting up while an interaction is happening. All members must be present during the interaction unless coming from a different area. Baiting your own faction members to intentionally cause a scene just to shoot without interaction is Fail RP.
📌 EXAMPLE: If you get pressed at Legion Square and it turns into a shootout, you CANNOT retreat back to your block. It is now a street fight. If you catch your opps lacking, you must have a 60-second interaction.
VehiclesInteractions cannot start inside a vehicle, you must get out. If a faction orders you out of your vehicle to start an interaction, you must comply. Driving off when someone approaches to start an interaction is Fail RP. If an interaction turns into a vehicle pursuit, the chase must last 30 seconds before you may shoot, and only to disable the vehicle, NOT to kill or injure. Chases cannot be brought back to block — they are street fights. You cannot stash items in a vehicle or anywhere else after an interaction has begun.

Drug Claiming
How to claim and fight for drug spots
▾
How to ClaimShow proof of all ingredients being collected and the drug being crafted, including locations needed for each ingredient. You also need 500 of the drug crafted with proof! Once claimed, the claim stays open for 24 hours. If another gang claims within that window, it becomes a turf-style war. If no other claim is submitted in 24 hours, the drug is yours.
⚔️ MANDATORY WAR RULES: 8 T-Count · No roofs or interiors · Only consumables may be robbed (guns only if agreed upon) · No vehicles after the war starts, no shooting from cars · The losing gang cannot run the drug or bench for 1 week · All members MUST have clipping software running. POV can be requested at any time, failure to provide may result in war forfeit or a rerun.
Taking to WarHave 5 interactions with the other gang at the drug location over a 3 to 5 day period (bench or ingredient locations — not just 1 day!). You must show proof of crafting at least 500 of the drug you're trying to claim (for example, if trying to claim Lean, show crafting 500 or more of it). If they don't show up to defend, provide proof of your gang going to the drug and making it known, either posting on Birdy or calling the opposing gang. You may take hostages at the drug location, but ONLY members already at the location — you cannot transport a hostage there. If the opposing gang still doesn't show even with a hostage, document it with clips.
📩 You must make a ticket with everything stated above to try and get a war started!

Drug Taxing
Tax limits, protection, and what you cannot tax
▾
ℹ️ WHAT IS TAXING? Taxing is when a gang controls a drug location and demands a percentage of materials or crafted product from players using that location. Taxing is NOT a free pass to rob everything a player owns.
WeedWeed is a civilian drug: gangs CANNOT tax weed. Taxing/robbery is limited to ONLY the drugs produced or ingredients at that location.
LimitsFaction Members: up to 50% max. Civilians: up to 25% max. If they refuse, you may rob or kill them (no flatbacking, proper interactions required). Only the gang owning the bench can tax at these locations! You must value your life if you are outnumbered greater than 3 (1v3+).
ExamplesHard Candy spot → only Hard Candy may be taxed · Cough Syrup spot → only Cough Syrup may be taxed · Lean Bench → Lean ingredients or crafted Lean Cups · Coke Bench → Coke ingredients or crafted Cocaine.
🚫 YOU MAY NOT TAX: Weapons, radios, or phones · Unrelated items or bank/cash (unless a separate valid robbery occurs) · "Double Dip", taxing and then immediately fully robbing the same person.
Tax ProtectionOnce taxed, a player receives 2-hour Tax Protection at that specific location. They cannot be taxed again by your gangs or chain-taxed across locations during this window. Example: Taxed at Lean Bench at 6:00 PM → cannot be taxed for Lean again until 8:00 PM.
ProofEvery tax interaction requires proof: screenshot or clip showing player name/ID, time, location, and items taxed, in the faction Discord under #factionname-tax. Failure to provide proof may result in items returned, scene voided, or staff punishment/strikes.
✅ A VALID TAX SCENE REQUIRES: Proper initiation and clear demands · Opportunity for the player to comply · High-quality RP interaction, no instantly running up yelling 'tax'.
Tax AbuseThe following are considered abuse: repeatedly taxing the same player, taxing more than the allowed %, taxing unrelated items, fake taxes to bypass robbery rules, holding locations hostage with no RP. Staff reserve the right to void any tax scene deemed unfair, excessive, or low quality.
OwnershipOnly the gang that currently owns a drug location may collect taxes from that location. Any gang attempting to tax a location they do not own is committing rule abuse. Drug taxes may only be collected in the form of the drugs or ingredients produced at that location.

Faction vs Civilian
When you can and cannot press civilians
▾
Valid ReasonsValid reasons to press a civilian: Wearing faction-affiliated attire (chains, flags, custom clothing) · Provoking or talking smack to faction members · Being caught actively crafting or selling illegal drugs or weapons. You CAN rob them and press them if they do any of the above.
⚠️ RANDOMLY ROBBING OR KILLING CIVILIANS WITHOUT VALID RP = IMMEDIATE FACTION STRIKE.
Civ RestrictionsCivilians may NOT press factions under any circumstances. Civilians are expected to value their lives and avoid provoking faction members.

Faction vs Police
PD response rules, raids, and interaction guidelines
▾
PD ResponsePolice will respond to faction-related shootouts with lights and sirens, and must make themselves known. Factions may NOT order police to leave the scene.
Your OptionsWhen police respond, choose one: Stay and Fight — must include verbal communication · Flee the Scene — escape to avoid arrest · Roleplay the Scene — comply and RP the interaction.
No Leading PDFactions may NOT lead or bring PD back to their block after an interaction or while being actively chased. You may not hold E in any scene with PD — if done so, disciplinary action will occur.
RaidsPD will provide a 24-hour warning before a raid begins. Boss stashes and shared faction stashes may be raided. Personal properties may ONLY be raided with a valid warrant. All illegal items found will be seized. All applicable stashes are locked once the warrant is served. You may sell drugs and weapons during this time but may NOT store, move, or hide them. Factions may be given the option to forfeit 50% of illegal possessions to avoid further escalation.
No KOSYou may NOT KOS (kill on sight) police officers, this is RDM. Cop baiting is strictly prohibited: intentionally luring police into an interaction solely to kill them. Police officers may be taken hostage, refer to PD SOPs for full rules.
GetawaysUsing a motorcycle as a getaway vehicle while selling drugs is strictly prohibited. You CANNOT jump or dive into water to hide or evade capture (from police or other criminals). Crossing water using a boat or proper water vehicle is allowed. Entering water to avoid capture = Fail RP.
🎭 MASKING YOUR IDENTITY, WHAT IS AND ISN'T ALLOWED:
NOT Allowed: Using a plastic surgeon to drastically alter your appearance to avoid arrest, evade police, or hide while under active warrant · Changing facial structure or adding age/wrinkles.
Allowed: Changing outfits · Changing or recoloring hair · Wearing a mask.
