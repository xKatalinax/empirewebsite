Formation & Recognition
How to get your faction officially registered
5 RULES▾
ApplyAll new factions must submit a formation request through Faction Support. Unrecognized factions cannot claim territory, declare wars, or invoke faction protections.
Min SizeA faction must have at least 5 active members before submitting for official recognition. Staff will verify activity before approval.
ConceptYour faction must have a clear, original roleplay concept and lore. Directly cloning another faction's identity or using real-world gang names without creative adaptation will result in rejection.
ApprovalRecognition is not guaranteed. Staff may request lore revisions, a probationary period, or a trial event before granting full status.
InactiveFactions with fewer than 3 active members for 14+ consecutive days may be dissolved by staff. Leaders are responsible for maintaining activity and notifying staff of gaps.
👑
Leadership
Leader rights, responsibilities, and accountability
5 RULES▾
AccountableGang leaders are directly accountable for their members' conduct. Repeated rulebreaks by members reflect on leadership and can result in faction strikes or suspension.
TransferLeadership transfers must be reported to Faction Support within 24 hours. Unofficial transfers are not recognized — the previous leader remains liable until confirmed.
LogsLeaders must provide staff access to faction logs, member lists, and internal communications when requested. Refusing a log request is a strike-worthy offense.
AbuseScamming members, using OOC threats to enforce IC agreements, or wielding leadership status to bypass rules are bannable offenses. Leadership is a privilege, not a shield.
LOALeaders inactive for 7+ days without a posted Leave of Absence may be removed by staff. Designate a co-leader or officer before any extended absence.

Membership
Joining, leaving, and loyalty rules
5 RULES▾
One GangPlayers may only hold ranked membership in one faction at a time. Being on two rosters simultaneously — including as a prospect — is prohibited regardless of how it's framed IC.
RecruitRecruitment must happen through in-character roleplay. OOC recruitment in Discord DMs, public chats, or community servers is prohibited and can result in recruitment bans.
BetrayalDefecting to a rival faction is a valid IC storyline, but must be properly roleplayed. Instantly switching gangs to feed rivals your old faction's intel is treated as metagaming.
ProspectsProspect or hangaround status must be recognized IC. Prospects do not carry full faction protection until patched in — but they are still bound by all faction rules.
LeavingMembers may leave with a proper IC roleplay exit. Leaders cannot force players to stay OOC or use rules as a trap. Peaceful departures must be respected.

Representation
Attire, colors, vehicles, and identity
4 RULES▾
FlagMembers must wear approved gang attire when operating as a faction. Random players cannot be treated as gang enemies based on clothing color alone — confirmed ID is required.
VehiclesFaction vehicles should reflect your crew's aesthetic. Full Faction Support approval is required before tagging any vehicle as an official faction asset.
TagsNo graffiti or in-game markers may be placed outside your approved territory without staff permission. Rival turf must not be tagged without active declared war status.
ImposterImpersonating another faction's colors, name, or symbols is a bannable offense. This includes planting fake recruits as spies or staging incidents to frame rival factions.

Territory & Turf
Claiming, defending, and limits on zone control
5 RULES▾
ClaimTerritory is assigned by staff based on faction strength, activity, and lore. You cannot declare an area yours by simply showing up. File a territory claim through Faction Support.
DefendDefending your turf from confirmed hostiles is permitted without a war declaration, but all combat rules still apply. You may not execute trespassers on sight without prior RP interaction.
CapNo single faction may control more than 3 active territory zones at one time. Exceeding this requires staff approval and triggers increased faction oversight.
NeutralCertain areas are designated neutral zones by staff. Open faction warfare, gang executions, and sustained conflict in these zones results in immediate scene termination and strikes.
GriefingCamping another faction's turf solely to block their activities — without an active war or story justification — is turf griefing and subject to immediate staff intervention.
⚔️
Gang Wars
Declaration, conduct, duration, and ending wars
6 RULES▾
DeclareWars must be declared through Faction Support with documented beef justification. Mass shootings between gangs without a filed war declaration are treated as RDM, not war.
BeefBeef must stem from in-character events. OOC grudges, Discord arguments, or personal player beef are not valid war justifications and will be rejected by staff.
KOSActive war status allows KOS between flagged members only. Uninvolved players, civilians, and unarmed members not actively engaging must still receive proper RP interaction before escalation.
DurationWars have a staff-set duration of 7–14 days. Staff may force a peace or extend based on RP quality. Wars do not continue indefinitely regardless of IC outcomes.
PeacePeace must be brokered IC and submitted to staff. Either faction may request a peace meeting — both leaders must sign off before war status is officially removed.
CiviliansMass civilian casualties, hospital camping, or targeting uninvolved players during gang wars results in immediate war suspension and faction strikes — regardless of which side started it.

Alliances & Beefs
Registering allies and avoiding super-alliances
4 RULES▾
RegisterAlliance agreements must be registered with Faction Support. Unregistered alliances are not recognized — allied backup cannot be called in during official war events.
LimitA faction may hold a maximum of 2 active alliances at any time. Building a dominant super-alliance is prohibited and grounds for staff-forced dissolution of the agreement.
Auto-WarIf one allied faction enters a war, the other is not automatically drawn in. Each faction must independently file for war involvement if they choose to participate.
StackingCalling in allies, off-roster players, or third parties to achieve overwhelming numbers is stacking — a serious violation. War fights must stay within numerical limits set by staff.
🏆
Faction Events
Airdrops, turf captures, Empire Games
4 RULES▾
EventsAirdrops, turf captures, and Empire Games follow event-specific rules posted by staff. General faction rules remain in effect unless staff explicitly overrides them for that event.
Fair PlayExploiting event mechanics, coordinating with staff for early info, or staging results is cheating. All event outcomes are final pending staff review.
PrizesEvent rewards are distributed after staff confirmation. Attempting to claim prizes before the official announcement or disputing results OOC may result in forfeiture.
HostFactions may propose hosted events by submitting a request to Faction Support at least 48 hours in advance. A staff member must be present for all faction-hosted events involving other gangs.
⚠️
Discipline & Consequences
The 3-strike system and how violations are handled
5 RULES▾
StrikesFactions operate on a 3-strike system. Strike decay occurs after 30 days of clean conduct. See the chart below.
InstantCertain offenses result in immediate dissolution without warnings: coordinated mass RDM, group harassment, staff impersonation, or exploiting server vulnerabilities as a faction.
MembersIndividual member bans do not automatically dissolve a faction. However, if 3+ members are banned for related conduct within 7 days, a full faction review is triggered.
AppealFaction strikes may be appealed within 48 hours through Faction Support. Appeals must include evidence and a clear argument — vague complaints will not be considered.
RebuildDissolved factions may reapply after 14 days with a new name, new lore, and demonstrated commitment to rules. Previous strikes do not transfer to a reformed faction.
