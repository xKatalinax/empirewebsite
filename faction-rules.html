📋 Formation & Recognition 5 RULES
ApplyAll new factions must submit a formation request through Faction Support. Unrecognized factions are not protected under faction rules and cannot claim territory.
Min SizeA faction must have a minimum of 5 active members before submitting for official recognition. Staff will verify activity before approval.
ConceptYour faction must have a clear, original roleplay concept and lore. Copy-pasting real-world gang names or directly cloning another faction's identity will result in rejection.
ApprovalRecognition is not guaranteed. Staff review all applications and may request changes, additional lore, or a probationary period before granting full status.
InactiveFactions with fewer than 3 active members for 14+ days may be dissolved by staff without warning. Leaders are responsible for maintaining activity logs.
👑 Leadership 5 RULES
AccountabilityGang leaders are directly accountable for their members' conduct. Repeated rulebreaks by members reflect on the leader and can result in strikes against the faction.
TransferLeadership transfers must be reported to Faction Support within 24 hours. Unofficial transfers are not recognized and the previous leader remains liable.
AccessLeaders must provide staff access to faction logs, member lists, and communications when requested. Refusing a staff log request is a strike-worthy offense.
AbuseScamming your own members, forcing IC agreements through OOC threats, or using leadership status to bypass rules are all bannable offenses. Leadership is a privilege, not a shield.
AbsenceLeaders inactive for 7+ consecutive days without a posted LOA may be removed by staff. A co-leader or officer must be designated before any extended leave.
🤝 Membership 5 RULES
One GangPlayers may only be a ranked member of one faction at a time. Being on the roster of two factions simultaneously — even as a "prospect" — is not allowed.
RecruitmentRecruiting must happen through in-character roleplay. Posting in OOC chats, DMs, or Discord servers offering gang spots is prohibited and can result in recruitment bans.
BetrayalDefecting to a rival faction is a valid IC storyline, but must be roleplayed out properly. Instantly switching gangs to give rivals your old faction's intel is considered meta.
ProspectsProspect / hangaround status must be recognized IC. Prospects do not have full faction protection until fully patched in — but they are still bound by faction rules.
LeavingMembers wishing to leave peacefully may do so with a proper IC roleplay exit. Leaders cannot force members to stay in a faction OOC or weaponize rules to trap players.
🎽 Representation 4 RULES
FlagMembers must wear approved gang attire when operating as a faction. Random citizens cannot be treated as gang enemies simply because they wear a similar color.
VehiclesFaction-associated vehicles should match your crew's aesthetic. Full approval from Faction Support is required before tagging vehicles as official faction assets.
TagsNo graffiti, in-game tags, or faction markers may be placed outside your approved territory without staff permission. Rival turf must not be tagged without active war status.
ImposterImpersonating another faction's colors, name, or symbols to cause drama or start conflicts is a bannable offense. This includes fake recruits sent as spies to collect information.
🗺️ Territory & Turf 5 RULES
ClaimTerritory is assigned by staff based on faction strength, activity, and lore. You cannot simply show up to an area and declare it yours. Submit a territory claim through Faction Support.
DefendDefending your turf from active hostiles is permitted without a war declaration, but all combat rules still apply. You may not mass-execute trespassers on sight without prior RP.
Cap LimitNo single faction may control more than 3 active territory zones at one time. Exceeding this cap requires staff approval and increases scrutiny on the faction's activity.
NeutralCertain map areas are designated neutral zones by staff. Faction conflict, gang executions, or open warfare in neutral zones results in immediate scene termination and possible strikes.
GriefingCamping another faction's turf solely to block their activities — without pursuing an active war or storyline — is turf griefing and subject to staff intervention.
⚔️ Gang Wars 6 RULES
DeclareWars must be declared through Faction Support with documented beef justification. Random mass shootings between gangs without a filed war declaration will be treated as RDM.
BeefBeef must stem from in-character events. OOC grudges, Discord arguments, or personal player beef are not valid war justifications and will be rejected by staff.
Active WarActive war status allows KOS between flagged members only. Civilians, unaffiliated players, and unarmed faction members not actively engaging must still be given RP interaction.
DurationWars have a staff-set duration (typically 7–14 days). If unresolved, staff may broker a forced peace or extend based on active RP quality. Wars do not run indefinitely.
PeacePeace must be officially brokered IC and submitted to staff. Either faction may request a peace meeting — both leaders must sign off for war status to be removed.
CiviliansMass casualties, hospital camping, or targeting uninvolved players during gang wars results in immediate war suspension and faction strikes regardless of which side initiated it.
🔗 Alliances & Beefs 4 RULES
RegisterAlliance agreements between factions must be registered with Faction Support. Unregistered alliances are not recognized — allied backup cannot be called in during official war events.
LimitA faction may hold a maximum of 2 active alliances at any time. Building a super-alliance to dominate all other factions is prohibited and grounds for staff dissolution of the alliance.
Neutral WarIf one allied faction enters a war, the other is not automatically drawn in. Each faction must independently file for war involvement if they choose to participate.
StackingCalling in allied members, friends, or off-roster players to achieve overwhelming numbers during a conflict is stacking — a serious violation. War fights must be within reasonable numerical limits set by staff.
🏆 Faction Events 4 RULES
EventsAirdrops, turf captures, and Empire Games follow event-specific rules posted by staff for that event. General faction rules still apply unless staff explicitly overrides them.
Fair PlayExploiting event mechanics, coordinating with staff-side players for early tips, or staging results is cheating. All event outcomes are final pending staff review.
PrizesEvent rewards (cash, assets, territory) are distributed after staff confirmation. Attempting to claim rewards before official announcement, or disputing results OOC, may forfeit your prize.
HostFactions may propose and host their own events by submitting a request to Faction Support at least 48 hours in advance. Staff must be present for all faction-hosted events involving other gangs.
⚠️ Discipline & Consequences 5 RULES
StrikesFactions operate on a 3-strike system. Strike 1: formal warning. Strike 2: temporary suspension of war/event rights. Strike 3: faction dissolution. Strike decay occurs after 30 days of clean conduct.
InstantCertain offenses result in immediate faction dissolution without warnings: mass RDM as a group, coordinated harassment, staff impersonation, or exploiting server vulnerabilities.
MembersIndividual member bans do not automatically dissolve a faction. However, if 3+ members of the same faction are banned within 7 days for related conduct, a review of the entire faction is triggered.
AppealFaction strikes may be appealed within 48 hours through the Faction Support ticket system. Appeals must include evidence and a clear argument — "it wasn't fair" is not a sufficient appeal.
ReformFactions that have been dissolved may reapply after 14 days with a new name, new lore, and a demonstrated commitment to the rules. Previous strikes do not carry over to a reformed faction.
