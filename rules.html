🛡General Rules10 RULES
18+Empire RP is an 18+ server. Adult humor and mature conversation are part of the environment. If you are under 18, you may not play.
MicrophoneA working microphone is required to play on Empire RP. You must use it while in city.
ClipsYou must run clipping software at all times (Nvidia ShadowPlay, Medal.tv, etc.). Clips must be at least 2 minutes long with audio from both sides. Reports must be submitted within 1 hour of the scene.
RacismZero tolerance for racism, sexism, or hateful prejudice. Derogatory slurs are an immediate permanent ban.
HarassmentNo harassment of any player, sexual or otherwise. Repeated pressure, intimidation, or OOC aggression is bannable. Any female disrespect or harassment will be escalated immediately.
TOSAll players must follow FiveM/CFX, Discord, and Rockstar platform guidelines at all times.
ExploitsNo cheating, bug abuse, mod menus, duplication, crosshairs, or PvP advantage packs. Permanent ban, no appeal.
ClothingDress realistically. No costume masks, animal ears, respirators, gas masks, helmets, or neon accessories. Non-PD/EMS/DOJ players may not wear those faction uniforms.
MonetizationNo selling in-game items, currency, or services for real money. No Tebex ownership transfers of mansions or businesses.
ConductStaff are not here to mediate personal drama. Revenge reporting and petty tickets waste everyone's time and will be penalized.
🧍Character & Conduct6 RULES
CreationFully customize your character before playing. Default models are not allowed.
NamesUse a realistic character name. Creative is fine, absurd or joke names that break immersion are not.
GenderFemale characters must use female models; male characters must use male models. Empire RP is LGBTQ-friendly and that RP is fully welcomed.
Multi-CharPlayers with faction characters may not run secondary civilian characters to escape faction punishments or super-civ.
InjuriesRoleplay your injuries. If you get shot, act like it. Injury RP is mandatory for everyone.
ActivityStaff may revoke ownership of businesses or properties not actively being used for roleplay purposes.
🎭Roleplay Standards12 RULES
RP FirstEmpire RP is a roleplay server first, a game server second. If you are offered RP, reciprocate it. Always be in character while in city.
QualityEmpire expects high-quality, realistic RP at all times. Staff may remove players who consistently break immersion or refuse to take the server seriously.
OOCNo OOC conversations in-city. Do not break character to discuss server rules, threaten reports, or talk about game mechanics. Open a ticket.
RulesplainDo not break character to explain server rules to other players. Handle rule concerns through tickets after the scene ends.
Report AfterIf a rule is broken during a scene, report it AFTER the scenario completes. Exceptions: racism, sexual abuse, or severe harassment.
/me CommandsYou must respond to /me commands from other players. Ignoring /me commands is FailRP.
Cop BaitIntentionally provoking PD into scenes without valid RP purpose is cop baiting and is low-quality RP.
Death RunNo death running or walking in circles during interactions. Have real conversations.
DrivebyThe driver of a vehicle cannot shoot during a driveby. The car must come to a complete stop before the driver may fire.
Body CamTo use video as in-character evidence, your character must be wearing a body camera or GoPro, or have a vehicle positioned for dashcam footage.
New LifeNew Life Rule: After dying, you cannot remember who killed you or the events leading up to your death for 15 minutes after revival. Wait at least 15 minutes before returning to the scene.
PowergamingNo powergaming. You must give other players real options for counterplay. Do not use emotes to exploit through walls or perform any action that removes another player's ability to react.
⚔️Hostile Interactions8 RULES
⏱ All hostile interactions must follow minimum time requirements before any violent action.
60 SecStandard areas: Hostile interactions must last at least 60 seconds before shooting or robbing. At least 45 of those seconds must be clearly hostile.
30 SecIllegal/high-risk areas (drug spots, money wash, black market, harvesting zones): A minimum of 30 seconds of hostile interaction is required before violent action.
2 MinPlayer-owned businesses: Hostile interactions must be a minimum of 2 minutes before violence occurs. You may not rob employees who are on shift.
Face-to-FaceHostile actions must begin on foot and face-to-face. Players may not use vehicles to skip the interaction timer or instantly escalate to shooting.
No PD ShotsYou may never shoot at PD from inside a vehicle under any scenario.
Valid ReasonsValid reasons to escalate to violence include: stealing someone's car, threatening someone's life, or aiming a weapon at someone. Boredom is never valid.
T-CountMaximum 8 players per side in any criminal conflict. PD is excluded. All hostile scenes carry a minimum 1-hour cooldown after the scene ends.
No CampAnti-camping: You may not camp spawn points, hospitals, police stations, or garages to hunt players. Any stakeout must have active, believable RP.
🔫Combat Rules6 RULES
RDMNo Random Deathmatch. You may not attack or engage in combat without proper, valid roleplay interaction first.
VDMNo Vehicle Deathmatch. Do not use vehicles as weapons. Repeatedly ramming a vehicle counts as VDM.
Revenge KillNo revenge killing. If you died and respawned, your memory was wiped. You cannot seek revenge until proper RP is re-established.
Third PartyNo third-partying. Do not insert yourself into ongoing shootouts or active scenes between other groups and/or police.
Warning ShotsIf you are caught in a shootout and officers arrive, you must fire warning shots before shooting at PD, then attempt to flee.
No RooftopsShooting from rooftops is not allowed.
🚪F8 / Combat Logging5 RULES
🚫 F8 quitting during active RP is one of the most serious offenses on Empire RP. There is no valid reason to disconnect during a scene.
No F8Do not disconnect during active roleplay for any reason — not because you were robbed, not during a chase, not during a police scene.
CrashesIf your game crashes, return to your last location and continue the scene. Inform PD or involved parties of what happened.
Item ReturnsIf someone F8s on you and you lost items, submit a 2-minute clip with audio from both sides. Your clip must not show you rule-breaking.
F8 Offenses1st: Warning + 100 Community Service + Pocket Wipe
2nd: 2-day ban + Pocket & Bank Wipe
3rd: 1-week ban + Character Deletion
4th: Permanent ban
Avoid RPGoing AFK to escape a scene, pretending to be unresponsive, or ignoring RP to avoid robbery are treated as rule breaks equivalent to F8.
📡Metagaming4 RULES
MetagamingMetagaming is strictly prohibited. Your character may only act on information they legitimately learned in-game. Using knowledge from streams, clips, DMs, or prior character lives is metagaming.
Ext. CommsNo external communication (Discord calls, party chats, phone calls) while connected to the server. All communication must happen through in-game voice and radios.
Voice IDYou may not voice-identify another player. If someone is fully masked, you must go through in-character roleplay to find out who they are.
Stream InfoWatching someone's stream and acting on information seen there is metagaming. Clip cutting or selectively-edited evidence will be dismissed.
😰Value Your Life (NVL)6 RULES
NVLYour character values their life. When outnumbered and/or with a gun pointed at you, you must exhibit genuine fear and comply.
1v1In a 1v1: you may try to defend yourself only if your weapon is already out. If a gun is already pointed at you, you cannot reach for yours.
2v1In a 2v1: if weapons are out and aimed at you, you cannot reach for a weapon.
3v1+In a 3v1 or greater: if weapons are out, you must fear for your life. You cannot attempt to escape or defend yourself unless your weapon was already drawn.
EqualIn equal-numbers situations, you may attempt to defend or escape, but you accept the risk of dying.
ConflictsOngoing conflicts have expiration dates. Continually engaging without giving the other party a realistic path to end the conflict is NVL and griefing.
💰Robbery Rules9 RULES
Bored RPNo driving around looking for people to rob with no RP context. Someone having a nice item is never a valid reason to rob them.
Pocket WipeNo pocket wiping. Do not take the majority of someone's items while they are downed. Taking phones, radios, pets, shoulder toys, wings, cuffs, or plushies is not allowed.
ScammingScam RP must be built on good roleplay. You may not scam for/with vehicles, houses, or donation vouchers.
VehiclesTrunk and glovebox robbing is allowed. Vehicles in green zones are off-limits.
No Rob ZonesNo selling drugs, running gun deals, or any illegal transactions in green zones.
Ocean DumpOcean dumping a player's body is allowed for story purposes but cannot force memory loss or a character kill.
Civ-LockedIf you obtain a civ-locked weapon after robbing someone, you may return it to the player for a fee of up to $50K, or open a ticket to return it for $25K compensation.
HitmanPlacing hits on players is allowed with realistic RP context. The targeted player must have prior knowledge of the hit through RP.
AFKBeing robbed while AFK outside a green zone is not valid grounds for an item return request.
🔒Hostage Rules7 RULES
WhoHostages must be random civilians with no affiliation to your group or faction. You cannot use friends or allies as staged hostages.
Condition AWith interaction (20-30 sec): Hostage takers must be 3:1 outnumbering the hostage. After the interaction, takers may draw weapons.
Condition BWithout extended interaction: Hostage takers must be 3:1 with weapons already drawn, clearly identifying the hostage-taking intent.
No EMSYou may never take EMS hostage under any circumstances.
LimitsMaximum 2 hostages per scene, 3 if conducting a Pacific Bank Heist. You cannot rob the hostage.
KillingYou cannot kill a hostage unless PD is present and it makes absolute RP sense. You cannot flatback a player after robbing them.
PD RightsPD may search a hostage for evidence during an active hostage scene.
🏪Business Rules6 RULES
UseBusinesses must operate as actual businesses, not personal stashes or faction supply chains. Staff may revoke ownership of inactive businesses.
TransfersBusiness ownership transfers require Business Oversight approval before any deal is made.
EmployeesEmployees must distinguish when on or off duty. Invoicing must be done properly; no billing abuse for personal gain.
No RobYou may not rob a business or its stashes because you were fired or dislike the owner. Hostile business interactions require 2 minutes of RP before violence.
Corrupt PD/EMSPD and EMS are not allowed to play corrupt characters without explicit permission. Open a ticket if you want to run that RP.
GZ SalesNo transactions or deals of any kind in green zones — including drugs, gun deals, and all other trades.
👑Gang / Faction Rules8 RULES
Flag UpFaction members must wear proper gang attire when representing their gang. You may not flag up until Faction Support has assigned roles and a current roster screenshot has been submitted.
Civs vs FactionsCivilians must show fear toward faction members at all times. Civilians may not act violently against factions unless provoked first.
RobberyFactions may only rob civilians if: the civilian refuses to pay taxes at a drug spot, initiates violence, or scams a faction member.
Spot ClaimsDrug processing locations are claimable first-come, first-served. Open a ticket with proof of knowledge, cash, and a clip of taxing someone.
TaxingOnly the faction that owns a location may tax there. Owning factions may tax civilians up to 25%, other faction members up to 50%. Same individual may only be taxed once per hour.
BeefGang wars require beef validation through Faction Support before becoming official. Gang leaders are responsible for their members' conduct.
EventsAfter any event there is a 15-minute peace time — no robbing or pressing anyone during this buffer.
AccountabilityGang leaders are responsible for their crew's conduct. Major rule violations can impact the entire faction's standing on Empire.
🟢Green Zones4 RULES
ProtectionGreen Zones are designated safe areas where players are protected from hostility. You may not initiate conflict or hostile RP in a green zone.
GZ LocationsGreen Zones include: Apartments, Hospitals, Cayo Perico Island Bridge, public gyms, City Hall, all legal civilian jobs, and active event locations during the event plus buffer time.
Build-UpYou may build up RP inside a Green Zone (except the Hospital), but the hostile scene must continue and conclude outside of the GZ.
Illegal JobsIllegal civilian jobs (e.g., chop shop) require a 2-minute hostile interaction before violence may occur.
🚑PD / EMS Rules8 RULES
Fear PDYou must fear PD as you would in real life. If police attempt to arrest you, you cannot immediately shoot them.
PD Use of ForcePD may use lethal force — you are considered a threat to society. If police do anything invalid, open a DOJ case.
ImpersonationNo impersonating law enforcement or EMS. Non-PD/EMS/DOJ players may not wear those uniforms.
EMS CallsDo not spam EMS calls. Press "G" to call EMS after dying. Scenes are considered over once EMS arrives.
RP with EMSYou must properly RP with EMS personnel. Do not ask for "taps" — roleplay your injuries. EMS may deny treatment if you are disrespectful.
Stealing EMSStealing PD or EMS vehicles, kidnapping EMS workers, or robbing/assaulting EMS is a serious rule violation.
RespawnIn PD scenes you may not hold "E" to respawn unless you are unreachable by PD, medical, or you are glitched.
DOJIf police conduct is invalid during a scene, handle it through the DOJ case system — not by RDMing officers or escalating OOC.
🌟Events & Airdrops5 RULES
Peace TimeAfter any event, there is a mandatory 15-minute peace period. No robbing, pressing, or hostile interactions during this window.
Airdrop KOSAll airdrops are KOS once you enter the marked zone. You may not shoot anyone who is outside the zone.
No Rob AirdropYou are not allowed to rob anyone at an airdrop location. Once the airdrop has been looted, combat must cease.
Cayo PericoCayo Perico Island is a full KOS zone. Flatback robbing is allowed. Factions may team up with their alliances.
Cayo RulesEven on Cayo Perico: No F8, No VDM, No slur usage. These rules apply everywhere on Empire at all times.
⚖️Ban & Punishment Guidelines5 RULES
StandardStandard ban ladder: 1st: Warning | 2nd: 1-day ban | 3rd: 3-day ban | 4th: 5-day ban | 5th: 1-week ban | 6th: Permanent ban
Perma BanImmediate permanent ban: cheating/hacking, exploiting/duplication, derogatory slurs, PvP-advantage packs. No appeal.
DiscretionEmpire staff reserve the right to make judgment calls based on the situation. If something leads to a genuinely positive RP experience, staff may choose not to intervene.
ExploitingDo not abuse in-game mechanics for personal gain. If something seems overpowered or broken, stop using it and report it.
Staff FinalEmpire RP staff decisions are final. All punishments are subject to individual circumstance.
