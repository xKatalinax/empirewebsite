🛡 General Rules 4 RULES
RespectNo toxicity, hate speech, harassment, or OOC drama. Keep problems in tickets, not in chat.
TOSAll players must follow FiveM/CFX, Discord, and Rockstar platform guidelines at all times.
ClipsReports require proof. Clip rulebreaks and submit through the correct support ticket.
ExploitsNo cheating, bug abuse, duplication, mod menus, crosshairs, or third-party advantages.
🎭 Roleplay Rules 4 RULES
InteractionEmpire is interaction first. Give players a real chance to respond before escalating scenes.
45 SecGeneral hostile RP requires proper interaction before shooting, robbing, or forcing a scene.
30 SecHot zones / red zones may use a shorter interaction standard when posted by staff.
NVLValue your life. If you are outnumbered, held at gunpoint, or trapped — roleplay the fear properly.
⚔️ Combat Rules 4 RULES
RDMNo random killing. Every shooting must have valid roleplay build-up or approved event rules.
VDMNo using vehicles as weapons unless the scene clearly supports it and staff rules allow it.
F8 QuitNo combat logging, F8 quitting, respawning, or disconnecting to avoid consequences.
ScenesDo not return to active scenes after death unless staff or event rules clearly allow it.
👑 Gang / Faction Rules 4 RULES
FlagFaction members must wear proper gang attire when representing their gang.
BeefGang wars require beef validation through Faction Support before becoming official.
EventsAirdrops, turfs, and Empire Games follow event-specific rules posted by staff.
AccountabilityGang leaders are responsible for their members. Cheating or major rulebreaks can affect the whole gang.
💰 Robbery Rules 4 RULES
CommsYou may take a phone OR radio, not both. Players must be left with one communication method.
FlatbackFlatback may be used only within allowed rules and never to protect friends from being robbed.
No RobNo robbing during events when staff posts "no robbing" for that event.
ProofKeep clips of major robberies, kidnaps, gang scenes, and disputes.
🏪 Business Rules 4 RULES
UseBusinesses must be used as actual businesses, not personal stashes or faction supply machines.
InvoicesEmployees must invoice properly and cannot abuse billing for themselves, friends, or gangs.
TransfersOwnership transfers require Business Oversight approval before any purchase or agreement.
ActivityOwners are expected to stay active, provide logs when asked, and follow oversight instructions.
