Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before connecting to the city.

How do I join the server? +
Install FiveM, join the Discord, read the rules, then connect using the Play Now button with your server link. It only takes a few minutes to get in city.
Do I need to apply to play? +
General access can be open, but PD, EMS, gangs, staff, and businesses may require applications or interviews. Check Discord for current requirements.
Can I start my own gang? +
Yes. Open a Faction Support ticket in Discord and follow the current gang creation requirements. Active rosters and RP history help your application.
What is beef validation? +
Gang wars must be escalated through Faction Support before going official. This protects both sides and ensures fair, story-driven conflict instead of random wars.
How are rule breaks handled? +
Open a support ticket with clip evidence. Staff review reports and take action based on severity. Repeat offenses carry harsher consequences.
