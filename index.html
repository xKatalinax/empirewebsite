Your Path Into The City

Four steps between you and your first day in Empire.

01

Download & Connect

Grab FiveM, join the Discord, then hit Play Now. Start to finish, you're in the city in about 10 minutes.

02

Gear Up

A working mic and clipping software (ShadowPlay, Medal, etc.) are required at all times. No exceptions, no text-only RP, no unrecorded reports.

03

Build Your Character

Skip the default models. Fully customize your look and read the rules before your first scene, it sets the tone for everything after.

04

Pick Your Path

Civilian, business owner, gang, PD or EMS, your story is yours to write. Use the links below to see what each route requires.
