Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before your first day in Empire.

🎮
How do I get into Empire RP?
+
Download FiveM, join our Discord, read the rules, then hit the Play Now button to connect. The whole process takes about 10 minutes. Once you're in, fully customize your character before doing anything, default models aren't allowed.
🎤
Do I need a mic to play?
+
Yes, a working microphone is required. Empire RP is a serious voice-RP server. Text-only roleplay is not how we operate. If you don't have a mic, get one before joining. This is non-negotiable.
🎬
Do I need to record my gameplay?
+
Yes. You must run clipping software at all times, Nvidia ShadowPlay, Medal.tv, or similar. Clips must be at least 2 minutes long with audio from both sides. No clip means no item returns and no valid reports. If you're not clipping, you're not protected.
👑
Can I start my own gang or faction?
+
Yes, Empire RP supports player-created factions. Open a Faction Support ticket in Discord to get started. You'll need to show an active roster, demonstrated RP history in the city, and meet the gang creation requirements. Quality RP and commitment are what get factions approved, not just numbers.
⚔️
How does gang beef and conflict work?
+
Conflicts between factions must be escalated through Faction Support before going official. This isn't red tape, it keeps wars story-driven and fair for both sides. Random shooting with no RP context will get you punished. Build the narrative, then bring the smoke.
🚔
How do I join PD or EMS?
+
PD and EMS require applications and/or interviews. Check the Empire Discord for current openings, requirements, and the application process. You'll need solid RP experience in the city before applying, show us you know how to play first.
🏪
Can I own a business in the city?
+
Yes. Player-owned businesses are a major part of Empire RP's economy and roleplay. Businesses must be actively operated as real businesses, not used as stashes or faction supply chains. Ownership transfers require Business Oversight approval. If you go inactive, staff may revoke the business.
🚨
Someone broke the rules, what do I do?
+
Stay in the scene and finish the roleplay first, don't break character to address it. After the scenario ends, open a support ticket in Discord with your clip. Reports require video and audio evidence from both sides, minimum 2 minutes. Reports must be submitted within 1 hour of the scene. Petty or revenge reports are penalized.
💀
What happens if I F8 or disconnect mid-scene?
+
F8 quitting during active RP is one of the most serious offenses on Empire. First offense: Warning + community service + pocket wipe. Second: 2-day ban + bank wipe. Third: 1-week ban + character deletion. Fourth: Permanent ban. There is no valid reason to leave mid-scene, if your game crashes, you must return and continue.
💬
Still have questions?
+
Join the Empire RP Discord and open a support ticket. Our staff and community are active and happy to help new and existing members. The city is waiting, come get your questions answered and get in.
