FAQ

Common
Questions

Everything you need to know before your first day in Empire.

01 How do I get into Empire RP?
+
Download FiveM, join our Discord, read the rules, then hit Play Now. The whole process takes about 10 minutes. Once you're in, fully customize your character — default models aren't allowed.
02 Do I need a mic?
+
Yes. A working microphone is required. Empire RP is a voice RP server — text-only is not how we operate. Non-negotiable.
03 Do I need to record my gameplay?
+
Yes. Run clipping software at all times — ShadowPlay, Medal.tv, or similar. Clips must be at least 2 minutes with audio from both sides. No clip means no item returns and no valid reports.
04 Can I start my own gang or faction?
+
Yes. Open a Faction Support ticket in Discord. You'll need an active roster, demonstrated RP history in the city, and meet the gang creation requirements. Quality RP and commitment — not just numbers — get factions approved.
05 How do I join PD or EMS?
+
PD and EMS require applications and/or interviews. Check the Discord for current openings. You'll need solid RP experience first — show us you know how to play.
06 What happens if I F8 mid-scene?
+
F8 quitting during active RP is one of the most serious offenses on Empire. 1st offense: Warning + community service + pocket wipe. 2nd: 2-day ban + bank wipe. 3rd: 1-week ban + character deletion. 4th: Permanent ban. If your game crashes, return and continue.
07 Someone broke the rules — what do I do?
+
Stay in the scene. After it ends, open a support ticket in Discord with your clip. Reports require video and audio evidence — minimum 2 minutes — submitted within 1 hour. Petty or revenge reports are penalized.
08 Still have questions?
+
Join the Empire RP Discord and open a support ticket. Our staff and community are active and happy to help. The city is waiting.
